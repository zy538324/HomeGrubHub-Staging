Refund Policy

At HomeGrubHub, we strive to provide excellent service and value to our users.

Subscription Refunds

Premium Subscriptions

  • 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee: New premium subscribers can request a full refund within 7 days of their first subscription.
  • Unused Portion: If you cancel your subscription, you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
  • No Partial Refunds: We do not provide partial refunds for partially used billing periods after the 7-day guarantee.

Annual Subscriptions

  • 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee: Annual subscribers can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
  • Pro-rated Refunds: After 30 days, refunds may be considered on a case-by-case basis with pro-rated amounts.

How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact our support team at support@homegrubhub.co.uk
  2. Include your account email and reason for the refund request
  3. Allow 3-5 business days for processing
  4. Refunds will be processed to the original payment method

Non-Refundable Items

  • Free account features and services
  • Downloadable content that has been accessed
  • Custom meal plans that have been delivered
  • Usage fees for API access

Special Circumstances

We may consider refunds outside of our standard policy for:

  • Technical issues preventing service access
  • Billing errors or duplicate charges
  • Service outages lasting more than 24 hours
  • Fraudulent or unauthorized charges

Processing Time

  • Review: 1-2 business days
  • Processing: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Processing: 5-10 business days (varies by payment method)

Need Help?

If you have questions about our refund policy or need assistance, please contact our support team at support@homegrubhub.co.uk or use our contact form.

This refund policy was last updated on January 1, 2024. We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time with notice to users.
